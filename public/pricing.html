Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans anytime? expand_more
Yes! Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle with no penalties.
What's the cancellation policy? expand_more
Cancel anytime with no fees. Your access continues through your paid period. It typically takes 5-10 business days to process refunds.
Do you offer group or student discounts? expand_more
Absolutely! Contact us for bulk discounts on 5+ subscriptions. We also offer special pricing for schools and universities.
What if I need sessions beyond my plan? expand_more
You can purchase extra sessions at $50 each, or upgrade to a higher tier anytime. Custom packages are available for specific needs.
Are there refunds for unused services? expand_more
For the first 7 days, we offer a full refund if you're not satisfied. After that, unused sessions can be rolled over to your next month.
How does Sleek Academia's tutoring work? expand_more
You choose a service tier, complete onboarding, and get matched with a tutor who guides you through your exam prep — whether that's NCLEX, the bar exam, CFA Level I, or CompTIA certifications.
What is the difference between Full Service and Partial Service? expand_more
Full Service ($400) includes a dedicated tutor, structured study plan, and ongoing support. Partial Service ($350) gives you tutor access with more flexibility and self-pacing.
Can I get help with just my essays or exam strategy? expand_more
Yes. Our Essay & Exam Guidance tier offers essay review at $10/page and exam strategy sessions at $100/hour — no full package required.
How do I get started? expand_more
Click the Get Started button on this page to complete a short onboarding form. We'll match you with the right tutor and tier within 24 hours.
